** How to output MySQL query results in csv format?

i.e.
SELECT asin as ASIN, ebid AS EBAY_ITEM_ID FROM ebay_items WHERE ebay_store_id = 3 AND status = 1 INTO OUTFILE '/tmp/asin_ebay_item_id.csv' FIELDS TERMINATED BY ',' ENCLOSED BY '"' LINES TERMINATED BY '\n';


** Celery worker running for RabbitMQ **

(ref: https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorials/how-to-use-celery-with-rabbitmq-to-queue-tasks-on-an-ubuntu-vps)

To start celery workers as background:

celery worker -A tasks &

OR

celery worker -A tasks -n one.%h &
celery worker -A tasks -n two.%h &

i.e.
celery worker -A clry_tasks.automations --loglevel=info

To stop workers:

ps auxww | grep 'celery worker' | awk '{print $2}' | xargs kill


** Selling Stats **
select a.category, count(*) as ct from transactions t left join ebay_items e on t.item_id = e.ebid left join zz__amazon_items a on e.asin = a.asin where t.created_at > '2016-01-01 00:00:00' group by a.category order by ct limit 50;
