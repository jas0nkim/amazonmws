##### revisers/repricers #####


## repricer - POPULAR ITEMS
## reprice amazon items (ebay listed item only) every 4,10,16,22 hours at minute 15 GMT (4 hours ahead from EST)
# (on odd days)
15 4,10,16,22 * * * flock -n /applications/amazonmws/locks/reviser_popular.lock /applications/amazonmws/tasks/reviser-inventoryonly-premium-popular.sh > /var/log/cron/reviser-popular-`date +\%Y\%m\%d\%H\%M\%S`.log 2>&1

## repricer - NORMAL ITEMS
## reprice amazon items (ebay listed item only) every 12 hours at hour 11,23 minute 5 GMT (4 hours ahead from EST)
# (on odd days)
5 11,23 * * * flock -n /applications/amazonmws/locks/reviser_normal.lock /applications/amazonmws/tasks/reviser-inventoryonly-premium-normal.sh > /var/log/cron/reviser-normal-`date +\%Y\%m\%d\%H\%M\%S`.log 2>&1

## repricer - SLOW ITEMS
## reprice amazon items (ebay listed item only) every 12 hours at hour 2,14 minute 25 GMT (4 hours ahead from EST)
# (on odd days)
25 2,14 * * * flock -n /applications/amazonmws/locks/reviser_slow.lock /applications/amazonmws/tasks/reviser-inventoryonly-premium-slow.sh > /var/log/cron/reviser-slow-`date +\%Y\%m\%d\%H\%M\%S`.log 2>&1

## repricer - SOLD ITEMS
