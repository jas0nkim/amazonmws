### currently listed items

## reprice amazon items (ebay listed item only) every other day (odd day) at hour 1, 17 minute 5 GMT (4 hours ahead from EST)
# (on odd days)
5 1,17 1-31/2 * * flock -n /applications/amazonmws/locks/repricer.lock /applications/amazonmws/tasks/repricer-premium.sh > /var/log/cron/repricer-`date +\%Y\%m\%d\%H\%M\%S`.log 2>&1

## reprice amazon items (ebay listed item only) every other day (even day) at hour 9 minute 5 GMT (4 hours ahead from EST)
# (on even days)
5 9 2-30/2 * * flock -n /applications/amazonmws/locks/repricer.lock /applications/amazonmws/tasks/repricer-premium.sh > /var/log/cron/repricer-`date +\%Y\%m\%d\%H\%M\%S`.log 2>&1

## reprice sold items items in every 2 hours
15 */2 * * * flock -n /applications/amazonmws/locks/repricer_sold.lock /applications/amazonmws/tasks/repricer_sold-premium.sh > /var/log/cron/repricer_sold-`date +\%Y\%m\%d\%H\%M\%S`.log 2>&1


## update item title, description, pictures once a week Saturday
